what do the functions in disabled do exactly? I know I have to move them into enabled and all but curious as to what they do before I go experimenting
Posted 10-09-2022, 02:04 AM
quickkennedy HUD Developer
@Aimrurt
broesel cross - enables broesel cross on non-minmode. basically unused.
control point borders - enables outlines around control points, mostly for comp players.
crosshair charge - charge meter below the crosshair for sheilds.
damage account - puts a second instance of the damage text on screen.
default cross - puts the default tf2 styled health cross on screen.
evehud aiming guide - aiming guide from evehud.
full scoreboard stats - returns rayshud styled full scoreboard stats.
speedometer - enables the speedometer.
transparent viewmodels - enables transparent viewmodels.
