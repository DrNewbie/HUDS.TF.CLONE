Description

This is an updated version of the original posted to TFTV on Jun 20, 2019. I had this file sitting on my desktop for a year *oops*

This hud was created in a dark era of hud development. People were releasing the same dark hud over and over again. I wanted to make a hud that I wouldn't fall asleep looking at-

Enter ZephyrHUD; I made it as bright as I could without blinding myself.

It has a few visual issues here and there, I am human, afterall. Feel free to toss feedback in the comments here.
